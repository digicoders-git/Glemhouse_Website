Glem House Consumer Care

Glem House Consumer Care is a fast-growing manufacturer and marketer of high-quality, everyday household and fabric care products. Based in Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh, we are committed to enhancing the cleanliness and hygiene of Indian households through our effective, reliable, and affordable range of cleaning solutions. Our operations are driven by a deep understanding of consumer needs, a focus on quality ingredients, and a dedication to excellence in every product we create. .

A Journey of Excellence

To become a trusted and leading household name in the consumer care segment across India by providing superior quality products that ensure a healthy, clean, and fresh living environment for every family.

Today, Glem House is a trusted name, offering a comprehensive range of solutions that blend effectiveness with affordability. We take pride in our heritage while constantly innovating for the future.
